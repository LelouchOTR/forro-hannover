Workshops & Classes

Learn authentic Brazilian dance styles from experienced instructors. Our workshops focus on connection, musicality, and cultural understanding - suitable for all skill levels.

We offer weekly classes covering fundamental techniques, partner connection, and popular dance variations. Build confidence on the dance floor while learning the rich history of Forró.

Beginner Friendly

No partner or experience needed - just bring your enthusiasm!

Cultural Immersion

Learn dance as part of Brazil's rich cultural heritage

Social Dancing Sessions

Join our regular social dances every Tuesday and Thursday evening! Perfect your moves in a friendly atmosphere where all levels are welcome.

Our monthly "Kaffee & Kuchen" dance on first Sundays combines afternoon tea with social dancing. We occasionally host live music events and our annual Forró Festival Weekend brings the community together for workshops and celebrations.
